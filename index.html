On Monster Hunter

The Appeal Of Monster Hunter

Monster hunter is a game series where there are an endless amount of aspects people get lost in. From the deep and intricate combat, to the absurdly detailed ecology lore, to the silly stories told by quest descriptions. Nothing else quite can match Monster Hunter in These many aspects and more. The systems that all exist to facilitate your gameplay experience may seem complex at first, but if you give it time to get used to them you will question how you ever lived without them.

The Gameplay Loop

The gameplay loop of monster hunter ultimately boils down to going out to fight and kill monsters, so you can harvest their parts and go out to hunt bigger badder monsters, or just to hunt the same monsters faster and better. The core of this loop is in rewarding your effort in bringing down these behemoths by allowing you to make better equipment from them. The Games have over time gotten better and better at this by increasing the amount of materials monsters drop, reducing the amount of materials needed to make gear, and streamlining the crafting menus, but ultimately the series is still the same as when it first came out on PS2

The Monsters

Possibly the most important aspect of the monster hunter franchise is the titular monsters themselves. The monsters of Monster Hunter are all designed in a brilliant mixture of speculative evolution, and fantasy. While the majority of the cast of mosters are heavier on the speculative evolution side of the spectrum, Elder Dragons are far more fantastical in their scale and power, reflecting that in their pore classical fantasy designs.
