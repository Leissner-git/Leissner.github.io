The Monsters

Possibly the most important aspect of the monster hunter franchise is the titular monsters themselves. The monsters of Monster Hunter are all designed in a brilliant mixture of speculative evolution, and fantasy. While the majority of the cast of mosters are heavier on the speculative evolution side of the spectrum, Elder Dragons are far more fantastical in their scale and power, reflecting that in their pore classical fantasy designs.
